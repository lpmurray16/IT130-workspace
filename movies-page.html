All-Time Favorite

As an avid Harry Potter fan it is no surprise I'd pick one of the films as my favorite. This one in particular has always been my GO-TO. The only movie of the series directed by Alfonso Cuaron whose vision was spot on and magical from beginning to end.

Recommended

SpiderMan: Far From Home (2019)

I enjoyed the sequel more than I enjoyed the original. It seems like the writers were listening to the feedback of fans and included more action and a more deep and rich story.
