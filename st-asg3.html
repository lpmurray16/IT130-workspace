A Little About Me

Born in Auburn Hills, MI in 1995 about a year went by before we moved to hometown of Cary, IL. I have two sisters, Haylee the oldest, and Maia the youngest. Being the middle child between two girls is just as wild as it sounds. But I usually kept to myself and never fought much with them (mostly because I never needed to borrow any clothes). I currently live in Crystal Lake, IL which is a hell of a train ride to school, but I am almost done with my degree and I am excited for what comes next.

Education

I graduated from Cary-Grove High School in 2014. I thought I wanted to be a Nurse so I decided to go to the University of Iowa to study Nursing. After volunteering at the hospital on campus I quickly realized that this career path was NOT for me. I then went to a community college in AZ for a year before deciding that I wanted to pursue a career in Computer Science! I have been at DePaul ever since.

Hobbies

In my free time I enjoy playing video games and working on my coding projects. I am currently trying to learn the React JS Framework. I love spending time with my friends and just tossing a baseball or football around. With work and school and God knows what else, I really cherish my free-time.
